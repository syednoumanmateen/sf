Types of Super Franchise - Retailer
Super Franchisee - Retailer
Investment of ₹5 lacs and above, access to 600 brands delivery to Local retailers vicinity of 10 kms, trade marketing activity within the region
Super Franchisee - Customer
Investment of above ₹1 lacs , curated list of products for stocking, delivery to local StoreKing Agent or directly to a household in the vicinity of 5 kms, Direct customer connect.
Super Franchisee - CFA
Investment of above ₹2 lacs, delivery to local retailers with help of StoreKing Feet on Street (FOS), access to 600 brands.
Super Franchisee - Brands
Investment of ₹5 lacs and above, One or two brands super stockist, delivery to local retailers. Any existing SF can upgrade to Super Franchisee - Brand
